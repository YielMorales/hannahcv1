My Story

A little about
who I am

I'm Hannah Andrea T. Trajano, a BSDSBA student at the Asian Institute of Management (Class of 2029), concurrently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration in Management Information Systems at the University of Houston. I'm also the elected BSDSBA-2029 Cohort Vice-President for AY 2025–2026.

My background is rooted in marketing strategy, event organisation, and financial analysis — with hands-on experience from my time as an HR Intern at Eight8ate Holdings and over three years as a part-time instructor at Kumon, where I ranked in the Top 10 Math & English Completers in the Philippines.

"I believe great outcomes are built through strategic thinking, genuine collaboration, and the willingness to lead with purpose."

— Hannah Andrea T. Trajano

At Enderun Colleges, I served in multiple leadership roles — including Executive Director of Enderun LIKHA (Enderun's Got Talent), Logistics Head for the A.R.I. Business Pitch Competition, and Creatives Department Head. I graduated with High Honors as a full-time academic scholar, receiving the SHS Leadership Award, Academic Excellence Award, and the A.R.I. Leadership Award.

Outside academics, I'm passionate about volunteering, event planning, music, fitness, and art & design. I bring the same energy and attention to detail I apply in the classroom to everything I pursue.
